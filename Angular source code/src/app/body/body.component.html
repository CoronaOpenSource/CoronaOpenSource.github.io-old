Corona Open Source Open source science and tech to defeat the Corona virus
Our globalised world is optimised for speed and low prices. It is not optimised for resilience. We might like to think of ourselves as islands but generic drugs and PPE that keep us safe are manufactured in India and China. Likewise test kits and ventilator designs are produced here and elsewhere. There are a myriad of connections. We depend on each other

This is an open call to all IP (intellectual property) owners to widely license or to release your IP.

 Show us and help us build your technology to defeat this challenge.

We're going to start putting up names of companies we think could be more effectively licensing their technology, making money and saving lives. They need to act now.
